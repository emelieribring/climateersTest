Introducing the
Climabots
Climabots protect your kingdom from attack and are used to colonize other areas. They need power from your power plant and will be colour coded to signify strengths. They will be made from recycled materials ideally, collected from around the map. A healthy population is required in order to survive in the game
We must  Act!
Climate change is a reality - and the time to act is now. The world has to be carbon neutral by 2050. It's the one and only chance we have to preserve our planet - for us and every generation to come.

What our game does
Climate action that is easy, fun and accessible
Redemption enables immediate climate action — no extensive know-how, change of lifestyle or big investment required. Collect a Climatz with one click, and funds are allocated for projects fighting climate change
Awareness by immersion
Being a highly immersive game, Redemption celebrates biodiversity, sustainability and responsibility
Education against misinformation
To advance in the Redemption game, users have to closely understand real-world issues, and gathering knowledge about the earth’s climate zones is a huge advantage in the growing game. At every step, we support the player with easily understandable educational material about the world’s climate that always makes the connection to the threats of global warming
A sustainable community
Political parties are not doing enough for climate change, and a few individuals playing a game will not change any of that — in the broader scale of society, climate action will only be taken if there are groups of interest that have a strong voice rooting for change. Redemption provides a networking platform for a community of like-minded people, focused on sustainability
