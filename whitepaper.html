Climate Kingdoms
Play for the Planet
Summary
Climate action powered by the first play-for-purpose game created on Hedera.
Aimed at everyone, Climate Kingdoms (CK) is set to be the first digital trading card game that focuses on the issue of climate change. Based on the mobile or browser format, players will own a virtual plot of land and a helper called a Climabot. The Climabots will become more powerful as the player's ecosystem becomes more balanced and the game's cards will represent real-life nature such as trees and leopards. The cards themselves will be created via the Hedera Guardian workflow tool, ensuring full transparency.
Players will collect cards for completing actions on the platform as well as purchasing them. The game aims to educate and raise awareness about climate change while providing an enjoyable gaming experience. Players will earn various benefits from collecting cards and progression, including animal naming, voting rights and real-life safaris. Ultimately the aim is to release a fully dynamic virtual world and in turn CK would like to own and manage its own conservation areas in the future.

Ecosystem services: Rangelands provide important ecosystem services such as carbon sequestration, water storage, and soil fertility. CK cards will highlight these services and the important role that the restored rangeland will play in by supporting the surrounding communities and environment.
Local communities: Rewilding will also have social and economic impact on the local communities that surround it. CK cards will feature funding for and information about local people, culture, and traditional knowledge, as well as their involvement in the rewilding efforts.

By featuring different aspects of restoration efforts, players can learn about the importance of rewilding and the role it can play in supporting ecosystem health and biodiversity.

For example, the perfect ecosystem for rangelands depends on several factors, including climate, topography, soil, vegetation, and wildlife. Generally speaking, rangelands require a balance between grasses, shrubs, and trees, along with sufficient water resources, to support a healthy and diverse community of plants and animals.

In terms of climate, rangelands are typically found in areas with low to moderate precipitation, ranging from semi-arid to sub-humid conditions. The topography of rangelands can vary widely, from flat plains to rolling hills and steep mountain slopes.

The soil in rangelands needs to be well-draining and able to support a variety of plant species. Generally, soils with a pH of 6 to 8 are best suited for rangeland vegetation.

In terms of vegetation, rangelands require a mix of grasses, forbs (herbaceous flowering plants), and shrubs, with varying degrees of height and density. The ideal mix of plant species will depend on the specific region and its unique ecological conditions.

Finally, rangelands should support a diverse array of wildlife, including mammals, birds, reptiles, and insects. The presence of large herbivores, such as bison, elk, or antelope, can help maintain the health and productivity of the ecosystem by controlling plant growth and preventing overgrazing.

Overall, a healthy rangeland ecosystem is characterized by a delicate balance of ecological factors that support a rich and diverse community of plants and animals.

CK will be the first game to feature a perfect ecosystem balance as key for progression.
Gameplay – Collect to Protect
Success in Climate Kingdoms will include a combination of strategy, exploration, and collection. Players will need to strategize to create a balanced ecosystem on their virtual plot of land, with the Climabot providing guidance, assistance, and protection from outside.
Exploration will also be a key aspect of gameplay, as players would need to venture out to different virtual locations to collect new cards and resources. These locations will be based on other real-world locations.
Collecting cards will be a central part of the game, and players will need to complete certain actions on the platform to earn them. These cards will represent real-life nature such as trees and leopards that also include educational information about their importance to the environment. In addition to single-player mode, the game will in time also include multiplayer features such as friendly competitions and alliances to encourage collaboration and teamwork among players.
Players will have the following options to tend to their plots:
Ecosystem Management: Players will have the ability to rewild areas of the plot by planting trees, reintroducing animals, and restoring habitat. They can also manage their own virtual ecosystems by monitoring water usage, managing waste, and implementing sustainable practices such as energy.
Card Collection and Trading: Players will be able to collect and trade cards representing different animals, plants, and ecosystems. Each card will have unique properties, special abilities, power levels, and rarity.
Gameplay - Other
PVP Battles: Players can engage in PVP battles with their Climabots, using their cards and strategic thinking to defeat opponents. These battles will take place in special arenas or in the open world on special occasions.
Quests and Missions: Players need to complete quests and missions to earn XP, in-game currency, and special rewards. These will involve tasks such as helping to rewild a specific area, collecting rare cards, or defeating a powerful opponent.
Clans and Communities: Players can join clans or communities to collaborate on rewilding projects, share resources, and participate in larger-scale challenges. These communities can have their own special challenges and rewards, such as leaderboards, badges, and trophies.
Clans
Player can play solo or form clans in CK to progress faster or just to be more social:
Join or create a clan: Players can create or join a clan, which could consist of a group of players with similar interests or goals. Clans can be formed based on geography, shared interests, or common goals related to environmental conservation. Clan leaders can bestow power ups and titles on clan members.
Clan challenges: Clans can challenge each other to complete certain objectives, such as planting a certain number of trees or collecting a certain number of cards. The clan with the most successful completion of the challenge will receive rewards or recognition. As well as battling against each other clans can also trade P2P with each other.
Clan chat: A chat feature will be included that allows clan members to communicate with each other, strategize, or share tips and tricks.
Clan rewards: Members of the same clan can earn rewards for helping each other out, such as cards or in-game currency.
Clan events: The game will periodically offer events or challenges exclusively for clan members. This will include special quests, competitions, or rewards.
Clans can ultimately help increase player engagement and retention. Additionally, by creating opportunities for players to collaborate with each other, the game might promote teamwork and cooperation towards a shared goal of environmental conservation.
Gamification
Gamification is key in CK and leveling up is a powerful motivator for players such as:
XP and leveling system: Players can earn XP (experience points) for completing various actions in the game, such as completing quests, collecting cards, and rewilding areas. As players earn more XP, they will level up, which would unlock new features and rewards.
Climabot upgrades: As players level up, their Climabot will become more powerful and gain new abilities. For example, a higher-level Climabot might be able to restore damaged ecosystems more efficiently or fight more effectively in PVP battles.
Card rarity and collection: Players collect cards of different rarities, with rare cards offering more powerful abilities or higher scores. As players collect more cards, they will unlock new features or receive bonuses, such as in-game currency or additional XP.
In-game currency and rewards: Players could earn in-game currency by completing objectives or selling duplicate cards. This currency will be used to purchase new cards, upgrade Climabot abilities, or unlock new areas of the game. Leaderboards and achievements: Leaderboards show the top players in the game, based on XP, Climabot strength, or other metrics. Achievements will also be added, which would reward players for completing certain objectives or reaching certain milestones in the game.
CK  Levels and Benefits
Level 1-5: At these early levels, players learn the basics of the game, including how to collect cards and rewild areas. Players also unlock their Climabot helper and begin to upgrade its abilities. Players unlock the ability to name their first tree as well as receive a small amount of in-game currency to spend on cards or Climabot upgrades. Whitelist spots will also be given at this level.
Level 6-10: As players progress to these mid-levels, they begin to specialize their gameplay, by focusing on certain areas of the game, such as card collection or rewilding. Players also unlock new areas of the game, such as different biomes or ecosystems. Players earn the right to vote on new areas of the game to be developed, as well as receive a special badge or title to display in their profile. They also receive a bonus for completing certain quests or missions, such as a rare card or additional XP. Players will receive a free Climateer at this level.
Level 11-15: At these higher levels, players begin to create Clans and earn more rare cards and unlock more powerful abilities for their Climabot. Players could also start to compete in PVP battles and earn rewards for their victories. Players earn the right to participate in a community vote on the next major feature to be added to the game, as well as receive a small trophy or plaque to commemorate their achievement. They will also receive a larger bonus for completing certain quests or missions, such as a legendary card or a significant amount of in-game currency.
Level 16-20: As players reach these advanced levels, they unlock the most powerful cards and abilities in the game. They could also gain access to exclusive areas of the game, such as hidden forests. At this point, players are considered experts in the game, and may be able to provide advice and guidance to newer players. Players earn the right to participate in a special safari event, where they would have the chance to see real-life animals and plants in their natural habitat. They also receive a unique reward, such as a custom-designed card or a personalized message from the game developers. Additionally, they earn the right to suggest new features or improvements to the game, and have their ideas considered by the development team.
Design
Each card features a high-quality image of a specific plant, animal, or other element of nature, accompanied by detailed information about its characteristics, habitat, and importance to the environment. The card will also include a QR code or other digital identifier that would allow players to access additional information or multimedia resources related to that specific card.
To add an element of collectibility, the cards are designed with different levels of rarity, with rare cards being harder to find or obtain. Additionally, the cards are themed around different ecosystems or regions of the world, allowing players to collect and explore cards from different parts of the globe.
Quests
The quests and missions aspect of Climate Kingdoms involve a series of challenges and tasks that players complete to earn XP, in-game currency, and special rewards. These missions would be designed to encourage players to explore the game world, learn more about sustainability, and engage with other players. For example;
Rewilding Challenges: These missions involve tasks related to restoring habitats, planting trees, and reintroducing species. For example, players need to plant 100 trees in a specific area to earn a special reward.
Conservation Challenges: These missions would involve tasks related to reducing waste, conserving resources, and implementing sustainable practices. For example, players need to collect 50 pieces of litter from a specific area to earn a special reward.
Exploration Challenges: These missions involve tasks related to exploring the game world, discovering new areas, and learning about different ecosystems. For example, players need to find a hidden cave in the game world to earn a special reward.
Climabot Challenges: These missions involve tasks related to training and improving the player's Climabot helper. For example, you need to win five PVP battles in a row to earn a special reward.
Community Challenges: These missions involve tasks that require collaboration with other players. For example, players need to work together to plant 1,000 trees in a specific area to earn a special reward for the entire community.
As players complete these missions, they would earn XP, in-game currency, and special rewards such as rare cards, exclusive items, and access to new areas of the game world.

 Education
Education is a key aspect of CK, including:
Ecological information:  The cards will include facts and information about the different animals, plants, and ecosystems that they represent. Players could learn about the various species and habitats, their characteristics, and their importance to the environment.
Climate change facts: The game will include information about the causes and effects of climate change, as well as ways to reduce our carbon footprint and mitigate its impacts. Players can learn about the different solutions that are being implemented to address climate change, such as renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, and ecosystem restoration.
Environmental challenges: The game can simulate different environmental challenges, such as pollution, deforestation, or climate disasters. Players learn about the causes and impacts of these challenges, as well as ways to prevent and mitigate them.
Sustainable actions: The game encourages players to take sustainable actions in their daily lives, such as reducing their energy consumption, using public transportation, or recycling. Players can earn rewards or bonuses for taking these actions in the game, and learn about their real-life benefits.
Environmental careers: The game will also highlight different environmental careers and the skills and education required to pursue them. Players could learn about the various fields of environmental science, engineering, policy, and education, and explore potential career paths.
